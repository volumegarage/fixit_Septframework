This beautiful home located on a Blountville Farm was recently remodeled to a modern kitchen complete with an open floor plan that leads into both a family room and dining room.

From the stunning hardwood floor, to the details of the fireplace that was customized to match the kitchen, this new home is warm, inviting and will certainly be the center of family conversations in the future.

Our first modifications included removing a partition wall to open up the kitchen and dining room areas

Once the space was opened up we removed the existing cabinetry and installed Diamond Intrigue all wood base wall cabinets. To accent the cabinets, there is also a 60" wall pantry and a broom closet with all-wood construction. The doors are a Paloma Maple in Sterling white with matching crown moulding.

The kitchen is accented by spectacular black-honed granite countertops on the island and walls with a glass mosaic backsplash. We also placed new sheetrock to smooth the finish the ceiling throughout the rooms and installed stunning brushed nickel lighting

With over 900 sq. feet of hardwood flooring, Mr. Fix It refinished the work and finished off the job with a complete makeover of the brick fireplace. The original fieplace was transformed into a attractive wood finish with a wood mantel, a decorative trim with matching crown moulding. The hearth and the side walls were also fitted with Absolute Black-Honed Granite to compliment the kitchen and serve as the centerpiece for this elegant new living space from Mr. Fix It Home Improvements.
