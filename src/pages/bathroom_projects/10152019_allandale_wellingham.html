This recent project near Allandale in Kingsport is another favorite. Imagine reading a book or preparing for a night out in this new bathroom.

When we began, we removed partition walls that were part of the old bath and installed new Porada soft taupe color solid porcelain on the floor. For the centerpiece, we installed on Ove stand-alone bathtub with matching tile backsplash.

Behind the tub, there's a new 66x36 styled shower designed with help from our client with a bench seat and 2 wall benches. The shower has custom glass with 1 end panel and 2 glass panels with hinged doors. Along the walls, we set vanity cabinets and installed Kismire ivory granite counter tops with under mount sinks all complimented with Delta brushed nickel plumbing for an elegant and timeless bath.
