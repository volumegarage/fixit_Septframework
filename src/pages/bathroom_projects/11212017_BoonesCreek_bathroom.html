There's nothing like a repeat customer!

Mr. Fix It is proud to introduce another custom bathroom remodel in Boones Creek, TN. This project included a complete reno of an old bathroom with new cabinets, flooring, tile, fixtures and extending a walk-in closet.

We also installed distressed five-inch hickory hardwood flooring proceeding into the bathroom with Dakota Rustic door that opens to a new master bath. The walls are sprayed with a complete texture knockdown and ceiling finish which sets apart the floors and the cabinetry.

The detail and quality are accented by plywood construction using Kraftmade birch cabinetry with extraordinary wood grains in the finish. This all sits on top of full body porcelain Atlantis tile for both the floors and wall in an almond bardolino color.

We enjoyed working with the client to create a custom linen closet and base cabinet too. The spacious shower has complementary Atlantis floor tile and a rain-head shower unit. To accent the lighting, we also lowered the ceiling and brought the skylight more into view and designed crown molding throughout to set off the final touch.

Call Mr. Fix It today and let's re-create a new dream bath for you and your family.
