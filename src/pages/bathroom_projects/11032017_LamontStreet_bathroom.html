Imagine your new bath by experienced pros at Mr. Fix It.

This recent remodel in Kingsport transformed 2 and 1/2 baths from a historic outdated design into a modern showcase that adds value to any home.

We started with Diamond Prelude solid wood cabinetry throughout with a coconut color with grey stone glaze. In the master bath, we installed a stunning quartz countertop in Santiago finish. The centerpiece is a grey stone porcelain shower with matching mosaic tile on the tub walls.

We finished the room with brushed nickel rain heads and shower fixtures along with Delta 8" series sink faucets.

The entire room is accented by elegant wainscotting on each wall. The second full has black matte granite countertops and a Kohler Archer Biscuit finish tub. The half bath was finished with a stunning butcher-block counter with a white vessel bowl with Delta bright brass single pole faucet and accessories and as a final touch an Orleans mosaic floor tile.
