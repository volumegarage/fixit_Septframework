Mr. Fix It Home Improvements is transforming bathrooms all across the Tri-Cities. Working with creative ideas from our client in Johnson City, we transformed this home into something amazing.

What began as outdated baths and a bedroom constructed in the 70s became an elegant modern living space that adds value to this immaculate home. Mr. Fix transformed a bedroom into a walk-in closet and an additional washroom with a hall entry.

We constructed a gorgeous glass shower with tiled niche, rain shower faucets and a 80″ x 44″ glass shower door. We used TigerWood planked tile throughout and a double vanity separate by a convenient linen closet to compliment the spacious new master bath.

A second bath was transformed into a stunning clean smaller bath with 12″ x 24″ floor tile, a shower decorated with subway tile and accented with a pebble mosaic niche. There’s Kraftmade cabinetry with granite countertops too.

Finally, a third bath was redesigned with a warm glossy tile finish for the bath and floors alike. We accented the work with an oil-rubbed bronze finish and new lightening.

Watch our video on Facebook and see how we converted old style bathrooms into elegant living spaces and added a walk-in closet and laundry room. Call Mr. Fix It today for a free estimate for your bath and visit our job gallery at http://www.mrfixithomes.com for more ideas.
