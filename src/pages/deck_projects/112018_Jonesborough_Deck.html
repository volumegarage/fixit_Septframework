Just in time for the holidays, Mr. Fix It Home Improvements recently completed a gorgeous screen porch, deck and outdoor sanctuary for a home nestled in the foothills of Jonesborough.

This one has it all too - if you want to stay on a screen porch - there's a place for that. If you're going to sit outside in the sun - there's room for that. If you plan on sharing family time under the arbor cooking steaks or soaking in a hot tub, there's room for that too!

There was a small deck that was on the home in the beginning. Working with our client, we envisioned a new 16' x 16' screen enclosure with a cathedral ceiling, solid deck flooring and vinyl siding on both sides with knee walls. Above, there's a 1x8" tongue in groove pine ceiling with accented with matching architectural shingles.

Outside, the deck has several points of interest and are matched perfectly with coverage that measures 16’x10’, 20’x8’, 12’x10’ and 2x10. There are solar caps with a luminous accent - all atop 6" by 6" post footings for a perfectly solid foundation.

The centerpiece of this project is a stunning arbor with scalloped ends, 2”x2”s on 8” center and 6”x6” post with footings.

To complete the outside addition, there is recessed a 3/4”x4’x8’ pressure treated lattice for complete underpinning. Unique accents also include black aluminum 3/4” black balusters with treated 2x4 top and bottom railing and solar caps for lumination accents at night. Ready to discuss your project?
