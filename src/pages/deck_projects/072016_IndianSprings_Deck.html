Located in Indian Springs, this new deck was completely rebuilt from the ground up.

What started as 16' x 12' deck was designed and crafted with the owner to result in a 30' x 18' deck with an open floor plan. It could be three decks in one and features a gable roof and a low slope roof with complete cedar ceilings.

Built on a heavy-duty load bearing tongue and groove beams, this deck is solid. The lower deck has round metal ballisters and 2 sets of steps to walk up to the main deck. There's plenty of room for outdoor dining as the grill has an open space away from seating areas.
