Do you have a deck that is old or worn out? Mr. Fix-It recently replaced a small deck addition in Johnson City. The original deck was 19×12, and the owner wanted a new addition that would support a Jacuzzi Hot Tub.

Our team went to work and constructed a spectacular new 19' x 26′ deck with additional supports for a 10′ x 10′ area with an added joist system. We special-ordered 20′ 5.4″x6″ floor decking to eliminate all seams for an added finishing touch.

We also removed vinyl siding and installed channel rustic wood on the main house wail and continued the interior by buildout out a 1″x8″ tongue and groove continuous cedar ceiling. There’s even an open gable on the roof with a cedar finish and nice mount and outset for a big-screen tv for those nights in the hot tub.

This custom deck also includes round black balusters with a sandwiched hand-tailing that is sturdy while walking up to an 8’x8′ landing for an outside grill.
