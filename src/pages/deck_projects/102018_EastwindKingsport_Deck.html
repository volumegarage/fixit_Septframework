A great home is better with a screen room addition from Mr. Fix It.

This recent job in Kingsport expanded a 18'x12' room to a new dimension of 18'x18' Screen Room. 18’x12’to start. The cedar accents include a 4x4 support post, 2x10 beams for room trusses with a 6/12 pitch. We used Architectural shingles to match the homes existing roof and added 3" knee walls with cedar framing and aluminum black ballasters for east viewing.

There's also an insulated ceiling with a gorgeous 1"x8" tongue and groove pine ceiling with covering beams and inside gables. Finally, to accent the outside, there's a Larson Tradewinds storm door with roll-up screen along with matching railing, and 44" wide steps - finished with new gutters and down spouts.

Fall is a great time to plan your new screen room. Cool nights, warm coffee and family gatherings will never be the same.
